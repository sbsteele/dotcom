What's included

RayV Channel Monitoring (RVCM) (“The Monitoring and Alerts Tool”)
RVCM is RayV’s real-time live channel and stream quality monitoring system. RVCM’s Mosaic view and alert system, allows both RayV and our partners to fully control the quality of the streams and the user experience in real-time. The Mosaic View is generated by quality sensors on each and every channel which we refer to as the RayV Probes.

Rayv Probes (“The Sensors”)
RayV Probes are control desk sensors for alerting and resolving network issues in real-time while also ensuring the network behaves as efficiently as possible. The Probes monitor in real-time a wide set of parameters which provide a full picture of the state of the channel and Grid. Parameters include:

  • Channel State
  • Video Received
  • Video Expected
  • Video Ratio
  • Video Framerate (FPS)
  • Video Bitrate
  • Audio Bitrate

RayV Customer Support Desk: Support and help end users in real-time
RayV Customer Support Help Desk is a 24/7 live e-mail and chat support service for handling end user problems in real-time. Provided as part of our fully hosted turnkey solution, our Customer Support Desk handles a wide range of end user problems, from streaming issues to credit card and processing difficulties. RayV also created a unique database of Q&A’s with over 1,000 documented responses to help end users and clients with any issues they may have in real-time.
