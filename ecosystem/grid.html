RayV Grid™

The RayV Grid is the worlds first telco grade HDTV Delivery Network over IP. Designed specifically to serve the world's most sophisticated content providers, we stream for telcos and networks tens of millions of hours of HDTV every month to millions of viewers in over 150 countries. RayV is the most robust, scalable and secure network for delivering high quality video on the web.

With over 30 points of presence around the world and over 5 million nodes, the RayV Grid serves as the HDTV network for some of the largest live events multicasted over the internet every year for some of the most sophisticated global over-the-top and TV everywhere projects. The RayV Grid supports the entire value chain from channel acquisition to delivery with HD quality around the world. The Grid has a strong operation and monitoring backbone providing real time analysis of video quality and viewing experience.
