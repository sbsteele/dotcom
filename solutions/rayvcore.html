RayV Core™

Multicasting Over The Open Internet

RayV Core™ combines the robustness, quality and manageability of IPTV multicast solutions with the openness, scale and interactivity of internet streaming solutions, creating significant new opportunities for all the players in the market.

RayV Core™ is based on the RayV Optimized Cloud Streaming™ architecture providing the scale and managed environment enterprises need to optimize the usage of their existing networks. It also extends video offerings to other networks without losing quality or control needed in managed solutions.

RayV’s streaming optimization algorithms allows operators to reduce the cost of Capex and Opex by up to 90% versus other streaming solutions. (learn more)

RayV Core™ serves:

Tier One Telcos – A robust Over-the-Top and TV everywhere platform that integrates with all the existing OSS and BSS systems already implemented.

Tier Two Telcos – RayV Core SaaS™ - Streaming as a Service provides a full IPTV TV ecosystem as a service, without the need for any heavy investment in Capex and Opex.

Existing IPTV Multicast Operators – RayV Core offers the ability to expand their TV offering to additional devices such as PCs, mobile and slates while using the existing encoding and streaming infrastructure.
