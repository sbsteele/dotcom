RayV CloudTV

A Comprehensive Solution for Over the Top (OTT) Broadcasting

RayV CloudTV is a comprehensive solution designed for content owners or licensors (local channels, cable channels, web channels, aggregators, live venues, talent agencies) and distributors (telcos, cable/satellite/webTV operators, CE manafacturers, portals) who want to monetize via online streaming and OTT initiatives to new audiences around the world across all screens, anytime anywhere.

CloudTV combines the power of the RayV Global HDTV Delivery Network and the RayV Ecosystem products with a set of professional services enabling our partners to launch online HDTV programming to millions of viewers around the world in a matter of days, supported by a wide variety of business models such as PPV and ad-supported.

RayV Delivers the Entire Value Chain

One of the main advantages of partnering with RayV is that we manage, own and operate all of the ingredients of the value chain. RayV provides unparalleled video quality from the RayV grid, exceptional interactive video experiences supported by the monetization and reporting tools delivered in the RayV Dashboard. In addition we provide 24/7/365 dedicated monitoring and support from our control center. This value chain is delivered with minimal investments in infrastructure and operational support.
