Customer Support

RayV provides Telco and broadcast grade support to some to leading networks and TV operators around the world. As such we maintain a 24/7/365 support and monitoring center providing immediate response to all needs of our customers and partners. To meet the different needs of our customers we offer three levels of support programs providing technical support, live monitoring and expert level 2nd and 3rd tier support to our customers around the world.

All of our customers enjoy free access via email and a guaranteed response time of 12 hours via email support. In addition you are welcome to access our user guides and online knowledge base at rayv.zendesk.com
