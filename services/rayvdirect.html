RayV Direct

RayV Direct is a turnkey solution for our customers and partners wishing to fully outsource the support to the end users. The services are provided from our state of the art 24/7 monitoring and support.

RayV Direct includes:

  • 24/7 end user support via email
  • 24/7 end user support via live chat
  • 24/7 user support via 1-800 number (optional via a third party)
  • Full management and handling of technical, content and processing issues.
  • Real time management of payment and processing for users in 140 countries offering 20 different payment methods
  • 24/7 support for monetary and payment issues.
